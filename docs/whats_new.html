Version 0.5.1

General Changes

In deference to the Python tenet of Flat is Better than Nested, the ControlShapesManager class has been removed, and the controlShapes attribute on Transform nodes replaced by regular methods such as Transform.setCtShapes(). See Controls for updated information.
