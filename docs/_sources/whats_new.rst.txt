Version 0.2
***********

Additions
=========

*   A wide range of :ref:`maths operators <Using Operators>`, spread across :doc:`paya/plugtypes` and :doc:`paya/datatypes`, with support
    for bidirectional mixing of values and plugs
*   Added  :meth:`~paya.plugtypes.attributeMath16D.AttributeMath16D.pick` and :meth:`~paya.plugtypes.attributeMath16D.AttributeMath16D.asOffset`
    to :class:`~paya.plugtypes.attributeMath16D.AttributeMath16D`
*   Added  :meth:`~paya.datatypes.matrix.Matrix.pick` and :meth:`~paya.datatypes.matrix.Matrix.asOffset`
    to :class:`~paya.datatypes.matrix.Matrix`
*   :meth:`~paya.plugtypes.attribute.Attribute.get` returns :ref:`more useful types <More Useful Query Types>`
*   :doc:`Contextual name management <naming_nodes>` with :class:`paya.lib.names.Name` and pipeline suffixes
*   Dodge unit conversion nodes with :class:`paya.lib.mathops.NativeUnits` and :func:`paya.lib.mathops.nativeUnits`
